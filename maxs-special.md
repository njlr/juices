# Max's Special

## Ingredients

 - 1 Carrot
 - 1 Brocolli
 - 200g Spinach
 - 200g Kale
 - 100g Kidney Beans
 - 100g Chickpeas
 - 200g Turnip
 - 250g Cauliflower
 - 1 Chilli (to Taste)

## Notes

 - If you can drink it, Max will be impressed. 
 - 1 Juice is 2000 calories. You won't want to eat anything else that day!
